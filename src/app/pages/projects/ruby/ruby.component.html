About

Relicioius was my final project while at Wyncode Academy.It was made in 2 shorts weeks after being exposed to ruby on rails for approximately 2 months.Relicious was a twist on dating websites in the era of online dating.It allowed users to match themselves with other users by restaurants they like giving an easy first date.

Ruby on Rails
  • Private messaging between matched users
  • Restaurant search page that returned venues by terms you searched(Food types, restaurant name etc)
  • Data obtained through foursquares api
  • User authentication/encrypted passwords through the gem Devise
  • User's had the option to upload images to have a profile pic.We passed the attachments through our servers validation before sending it to a AWS s3 bucket where they're hosted
Challenges

The biggest challenge was the time limit we had and knowing we would have to present our application to almost 100 people.
The second was once we came up with the idea my partner asked me to create User registration and private messaging.I had no idea how
I would even begin to implement it but after a 30+ hour coding spree and many bugs I finished building a stable foundation for our app.
